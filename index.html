Project: GIS Program Modernization & Management (Bahamas Power & Light)

Led the strategic overhaul and ongoing management of the organization's GIS, migrating from legacy systems to a modern, cloud-based enterprise environment to support asset management, field operations, and engineering.

Key Initiatives & Developments

  • Migrated from ArcGIS Desktop to a cloud-based ArcGIS Pro database.
  • Created BPL's first publicly accessed application: an Outage Management Tracker (Hurricane Matthew 2016).
  • Executed extensive field data collection and database creation for New Providence, Eleuthera, Bimini, Exuma, and more.
  • Standardized data by adding domain values and intuitive symbology.
  • Provided continual database management, administration, and technical support for all end-users.

Organizational Impact

  • Asset Management: Enabled efficient locating and record-keeping for all BPL infrastructure.
  • Field Operations: Allowed teams to find assets with ease, measure cable runs from their desk, and simplify switching programs.
  • Engineering & Planning: Enabled load simulations to forecast future events, optimize generator/transformer placement, and manage load shedding.
  • Fleet Management:Provided the foundation to distribute teams effectively, reducing losses and customer downtime, especially during natural disasters.
